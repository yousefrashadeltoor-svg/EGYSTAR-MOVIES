Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how EGYSTAR MOVIES uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our platform. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

Our Cookie Usage

EGYSTAR MOVIES' Own Cookies: Our platform itself does NOT use traditional HTTP cookies for tracking purposes. Instead, we utilize browser localStorage technology to store essential user preferences and session information locally on your device. This includes:

  • Authentication status (whether you are logged in)
  • Language preference (English or Arabic)
  • Display name and profile picture
  • Interface customization settings
  • User agreement acceptance status

Third-Party Cookies

When you access external websites through our Platform's links, those external sites MAY set their own cookies and use various tracking technologies. EGYSTAR MOVIES has NO control over third-party cookies and cannot prevent external sites from setting them. Common types of third-party cookies include:

  • Analytics Cookies: Used by external sites to track visitor behavior
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to serve targeted advertisements
  • Social Media Cookies: Set when sharing content to social platforms
  • Functional Cookies: Remember settings on external sites

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, delete existing cookies, and set preferences for different types of cookies
  • Ad Blockers: Using browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Brave Browser's built-in blocking can prevent many tracking cookies
  • Private Browsing: Using private/incognito mode can limit persistent cookies
  • DNS Settings: Configuring DNS filtering (like AdGuard DNS) can block tracking domains at network level

Recommendations

For the safest and most private browsing experience when using external streaming sites, we strongly recommend:

  • Using Brave Browser or Firefox with enhanced tracking protection
  • Installing uBlock Origin extension for comprehensive ad and tracker blocking
  • Configuring DNS to AdGuard (94.140.14.14 / 94.140.15.15) for network-level filtering
  • Regularly clearing browser cookies and cache
  • Being cautious about clicking on suspicious links or pop-ups
